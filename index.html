Nebulae

What are Nebulae?

Nebulae are large clouds of cosmic gas and dust in space.

How are Nebulae formed?

Nebulae are formed when a star collapses on itself causing an explosion

What do Nebulae do?

Nebulae help make planets and stars from gas and cosmic dust, this also gives them the name "Cosmic Nurseries"

types of Nebulae

there are 6 types of Nebulae

Emission Nebula
Dark Nebula
Supernova Remnant
Reflection Nebula
Planetary Nebula
Diffuse Nebulae
